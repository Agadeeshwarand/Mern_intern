what is strictmode?

StrictMode is a development-only tool that identifies potential issues like deprecated lifecycle methods, improper useEffect usage, and side effects. It prepares your app for future features like concurrent rendering. It has no impact on production and helps ensure robust, modern code.

what is the cost of buying whatsapp?

WhatsApp was acquired by Facebook (now Meta) in February 2014 for a total cost of $19 billion. The deal included: $4 billion in cash, $12 billion in Facebook stock, $3 billion in restricted stock units to be granted to WhatsApp's founders and employees over four years.
